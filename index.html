mateus domingos

I'm an artist and writer working in Leicester, UK. My work has included games, 3d printing, fictional alphabets, maps and filmmaking.

I'm interested in exploring narrative, place, text and language. I run a meetup for Leicester-based artists called FTP, at Phoenix.

I recently published a short text and board game in collaboration with mathematician Hipolito Treffinger as part of MmATT at Leicester University.

I've been making print 'n' play games, bots, thinking about the decentralised web, fiction, releasing music, 3d printing and painting walls.
